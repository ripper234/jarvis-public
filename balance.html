☀️ Daily Rituals

Start each day with a personalized checklist. Check in with yourself, breathe, set a goal, start with a song. Each completed task earns 🌈 points. Tabs for Core, Bonus, and Good Night routines.

+5 🌈 per completed task

📊 Cycle Tracking

Track your bipolar cycles over time. See your current day, cycle length, and percentage of good days. Balance has tracked 15 cycles with an average length of 23.7 days, helping identify patterns that matter.

Know your patterns

🎯 Sprint Accountability

14-day sprints with buddies. Track check-ins, streaks, and personal bests together. Your accountability partners see your progress, and you see theirs. Nobody walks alone.
