WarmRoute

Meet the right people wherever you land.

What it is

WarmRoute is a trip-triggered relationship-routing service. You give it a city, dates, intent, and constraints. It gives you a target map, warm paths through real connectors, an outreach pack, and a final meeting plan.

It is an AI-native premium service, not a travel app: a human-in-the-loop concierge that converts a short trip into a very small number of warm, high-trust meetings.

Who it is for

Founders, investors, and tech operators with strong networks, scarce time, and high trust sensitivity. People whose limiting factor is not access to the internet but the cost of taste, trust, memory, and coordination.

Why now

High-agency travelers do not want another networking tool. They want a completed outcome: fewer, better meetings during a narrow city window. AI now makes a small team capable of doing the research, synthesis, drafting, memory, and coordination that previously made this kind of concierge service too expensive to run well.

How it works

🎯
Intake
City + dates + intent + constraints. What kind of meetings would make this trip worth it?
🔍
Research and targeting
Ranked target map based on trip goals, existing connections, and local ecosystem.
🤝
Connector sourcing
Warm paths through real trust graphs. Specific asks, not broad "anyone in Tokyo?" blasts.
✉️
Outreach
Drafted introductions, always human-approved before sending. No automated cold outreach.
📅
Scheduling
Calendar coordination with cancellation safety. One anchor meeting per available day at most.
🧠
Memory
Post-meeting follow-up and relationship capture. Every sprint leaves behind reusable assets.

Core metric

Operator-hours per successful meeting = total logged operator time for a trip ÷ meetings that actually happened and met the quality bar.

🏁
Pilot target
≤5 hours per successful meeting
⚡
Working product
2-3 hours per successful meeting

Privacy principles

No LinkedIn scraping or unauthorized automation. No blanket inbox ingestion. No auto-sending messages without human approval. Short default retention, explicit save for relationship memory.

What we will not build yet

No native app. No open social graph. No public marketplace. No automated cold outreach. Build the service spine first, then decide what becomes software.

First pilot

Japan, October 2026. Tokyo-first, family-protected. Goal: 2-3 genuinely worth-it meetings while keeping the family trip intact.

Status

Concept phase (v0.3). Service design and operator economics defined. First pilot sprint planned.
