You're not alone in this journey

Living with bipolar is like navigating a storm — sometimes the sun shines, sometimes there's lightning. But what if you could sense the weather changing before it arrives?

What is Balance?

Balance is the first tool that truly understands how you feel. Not just another mood tracker — a digital partner that learns your patterns and helps you stay one step ahead.

🧠
Personal AI
An algorithm that learns your unique cycles and alerts you to changes before you even feel them
📊
Smart Tracking
Sleep, energy, mood — all in one place, without turning it into another item on your to-do list
🎯
Daily Intention
Every day starts with the right intention — whether it's a good day, a hard day, or something in between
🤝
Real Support
Not just data — genuine understanding of what you're going through and practical help in real time

You know that feeling

When you wake up in the morning and don't know which "you" you'll be today. When you're afraid of the moment things start to unravel again. When you just want to live, not survive.

Balance was built for those moments.

How does it work?

1. Simple Logging
5 minutes morning and evening — how you slept, how you feel, your energy level
2. AI That Learns
The system identifies patterns you haven't even noticed and begins predicting changes
3. Daily Guidance
Personalized recommendations for each day — what to do, how to prepare, when to ask for help
