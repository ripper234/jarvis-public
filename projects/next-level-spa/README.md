# Next-Level Spa in Tel Aviv

*A world-class hot/cold therapy spa for Tel Aviv.*

## What it is

A spa inspired by Japan and French Alps experiences, designed around hot/cold contrast, recovery, calm, and excellent taste.

The concept includes:
- cold pool
- hot pool
- dry sauna
- top-tier massage
- meditation-friendly design
- high-end wellness tech

## Core idea

Ron believes Tel Aviv still lacks a truly world-class version of this experience.

The goal is not just "another spa". It is a place that feels unusually good, thoughtfully designed, and worthy of becoming a real destination.

## Why it could work

- wellness demand is strong
- hot/cold recovery is increasingly mainstream
- design quality is often mediocre in existing options
- a differentiated, premium experience could stand out sharply

## Experience principles

- beautiful and calming physical environment
- strong attention to ritual and flow
- excellent service
- real quality, not gimmicks
- room for both recovery and contemplation

## Open questions

- membership model vs pay-per-visit
- central Tel Aviv vs slightly outside the city
- what level of wellness tech actually improves the experience
- whether the best first version is boutique and premium or broader and more accessible

## Status

Early-stage concept. This belongs in the Wish Machine / Request for Startups universe: a thing Ron wants to exist, whether or not he is the builder.
