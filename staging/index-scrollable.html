📋Methodology

Ron writes specs, Jarvis does the work — websites, research, PRs, monitoring — and reports back each morning. Ron reviews in ~30 minutes. The agent proposes, never acts alone. 10 interruptions/day max. Safety enforced by code, not prompts. "Think deeply, output lightly."

Agent as Co-Founder

Ron writes specifications, not prompts. Jarvis reads specs and proposes tiny improvements as PRs, 1-2 lines at a time.

Attention as Currency

10 free + 20 earnable credits daily. Capture mode costs zero. Only the final summary costs attention.

Safety by Architecture

No direct write access. Every mutation goes through a code-level guard. Rules live in scripts, not prompts.

Spec is the Product

Top-down: spec first, then tests, then architecture, then code. "Think deeply, output lightly."

SOUL.md (personality), USER.md (your info), MEMORY.md (long-term memory).
